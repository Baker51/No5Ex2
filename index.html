A&P Page 1

...This above page was done in InDesign and it's an example of the work I did while at A&P Corporate Offices in Montvale, NJ.
My job of many was to version out each page 1 depending on the sales program allowed for those particular stores. For example, some stores would get Boars Head Deli (top of page, instead of Hormel Deli) or a Liquor or Beer Item (One added item next to the Shampoo block, 4 across) or a certain price point or structure as well, (Coca-Cola is 5/$5 instead of 4/$5.)
In this case above, this version was the Base Version so it was pretty "basic" but extremely important to the success of the weeks advertising goal. The Base version was the first version made and others were made off of this one.
